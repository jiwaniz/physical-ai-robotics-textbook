# Introduction to Physical AI

Welcome to the introduction module! This section covers the foundations of Physical AI and prepares you for the 13-week journey ahead.

## What You'll Learn

### Week 1: Foundations of Physical AI
- What is Physical AI and embodied intelligence
- The role of robotics in AI systems
- Course overview and learning objectives
- Hardware and software prerequisites

### Week 2: Sensors and Perception
- Introduction to robot sensors
- Perception systems for physical environments
- Setting up your development environment
- Introduction to ROS 2 ecosystem

## Module Objectives

By the end of this introduction module, you will:
- ✅ Understand the concept of Physical AI and embodied intelligence
- ✅ Set up your development environment (Ubuntu 22.04, ROS 2 Humble)
- ✅ Understand the course structure and assessment criteria
- ✅ Be ready to dive into ROS 2 programming

## Prerequisites

Before starting this course, you should have:
- Basic Python programming knowledge
- Familiarity with Linux command line
- Understanding of AI/ML concepts (neural networks, training)
- (Optional) Access to RTX GPU or Jetson hardware
